Boa Hancock

One of the Seven Warlords of the Sea. Empress of "the Island of Women. She always looks down on and acts superior to others.

Nami

Nami is the Straw Hat Pirates' navigator, who dreams of drawing a map of the entire world.

Shirahoshi

Princess Shirahoshi, also known as Mermaid Princess. She is a giant smelt-whiting mermaid and the princess of the Ryugu Kingdom.

Uta

Uta is a world-famous singer or diva and the adopted daughter of Red-Haired Shanks. She was also a musician in his crew.

Nico Robin

Nico Robin, also known by her epithet "Devil Child" is the archaeologist of the Straw Hat Pirates and one of the Senior Officers of the Straw Hat Grand Fleet.

Ulti

Ulti is one of the Tobiroppo, the six strongest Shinuchi of the Beasts Pirates. She is the elder sister of fellow Tobiroppo Page One.

Stussy

Stussy is MADS' first-ever successful clone, having been modeled after Miss Buckingham Stussy. She is a spy in the employ of Dr. Vegapunk.

Hiyori

Hiyori is a beautiful and slim woman who has a strong resemblance of her mother, Kozuki Toki.
